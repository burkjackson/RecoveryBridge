Common Questions

Is this really anonymous?
Yes. We prioritize privacy. You decide what you share, and personal contact info is never required. All conversations are encrypted.
Who are the peer supporters?
They're people in stable recovery who've completed onboarding and agreed to clear guidelines. Real people who understand the journey.
Is this therapy or professional help?
No. RecoveryBridge is peer support, not professional therapy or crisis intervention. We provide connection between people in recovery.
Will this always be free?
Yes. RecoveryBridge will always be free. No paywalls, no premium features. Recovery support should be accessible to everyone.
